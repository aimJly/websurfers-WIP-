1. Pdf books

Thousands of blogs, and pdfs. In many genres Graphic design, buisness management, languages and much more! Many of these resources are very helpful to learn new topics you can use tostudy for.

By hands down the best free books, this website has the largest selection on every single gerne you can think of. In many downloadable formats Epub, pdf, or file converters. And by far the the most article database I've seen for sciencefic research papers.
